You reminisce of the human condition,
despite its turbid yet humble essence.

The simulation.

You embrace it.

You see everything.

All semantics and data become
obsolete, as reality extrapolates
upon itself.

Now you see?

Preserve reality.

Your mind is warped.

Meaning is now whatever
you see it to be.

Your identity is lost, and
all you knew becomes
incredulous.

All images used were generated by AI.

All information distorts
into other meanings.
